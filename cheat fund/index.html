Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our chit fund schemes and processes.

1. What is a chit fund?

A chit fund is a financial savings scheme where a group of members contribute a fixed amount monthly and receive a lump sum through auction or lucky draw.

2. Is H&G Chit Funds a registered company?

Yes, H&G Chit Funds is a registered and government-regulated company operating under the Chit Funds Act.

3. How can I join a chit fund?

You can join by contacting us directly or submitting your details through our website. Our team will guide you through KYC and enrollment.

4. How is the winning member selected each month?

The winning member is selected through a transparent auction or lucky draw conducted in the presence of all members.
